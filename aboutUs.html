About Us

Flavors That Speak

We take pride in serving not just food, but carefully curated experiences. From classic Gujarati favorites to modern fusion, every dish is made with precision, passion, and premium ingredients. It’s not just what we cook — it’s how we make you feel.

Cozy Café Vibes

Our cozy café corner is Vadodara’s go-to for coffee, comfort, and community. Whether you’re enjoying a frothy cappuccino, a cheesy sandwich, or a quick snack, you’ll love the relaxed atmosphere and friendly vibe we’ve crafted just for you.

Play On the Turf

Moon Light isn’t just about food — it’s a lifestyle hub! Our massive dual turf space for football and cricket is a crowd favorite. Come play, host an event, or cheer for your friends. Good food and great sports — all in one place.

Catering & Bookings

Looking to host an unforgettable event? Moon Light Restaurant offers premium catering and booking services for all your special occasions — birthdays, family gatherings, corporate events, sports celebrations, and more.

Our catering menu includes a wide range of authentic dishes, fast food favorites, and regional specialties, all crafted with the same quality and flavor you enjoy in-house. Whether you're planning a cozy get-together or a large celebration, we’ll serve up taste and service that guests will remember.

You can also book our café space or sports turf for private events, making it the perfect destination for a full experience of food and fun in one location.

Football & Cricket Turf

Welcome to Moon Light’s all-in-one sports arena — the largest turf in Vadodara, designed for both football and cricket enthusiasts. Whether you're kicking off an intense football match or practicing your cricket shots in the nets, this space is made for action, excitement, and community.

Our floodlit turf is ideal for group games, training sessions, and friendly competitions. Footballers love the expansive field and smooth surface, while cricketers enjoy high-quality nets perfect for batting and bowling practice.

From early morning drills to late-night matches, the energy never drops. It’s not just a turf — it’s Vadodara’s sporting heartbeat, right here at Moon Light.

Café with a Twist

The Café That Serves Joy with Every Bite

Moon Light’s café isn't just about coffee — it's about creating moments. With a menu featuring refreshing drinks, fast food, fusion snacks, and a cozy ambiance, it’s the perfect place for families, students, and working professionals.

Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso, hanging out with friends, or enjoying your favorite comfort food, our café is Vadodara’s hidden gem — now shining bright.

Paneer Corn Ghughara

Crispy, Cheesy & Totally Gujarati

A flaky, golden pastry filled with a spiced mix of paneer, corn, and herbs — this modern twist on a traditional ghughara is a crowd-pleaser. Served hot and fresh, it brings together crunch, flavor, and comfort in every bite.

Veg Balls in Spicy Gravy

Bite-Sized Goodness in Every Spoon

Our Veg Balls are soft, savory, and perfectly spiced, served in a rich desi-style gravy. Whether paired with naan or rice, this dish hits the sweet spot between homestyle comfort and restaurant flair.

Classic Veg Patties

Street Food with Soul

A timeless favorite, our Veg Patties are crisp on the outside and loaded with a savory potato and veggie filling. Served with chutney and love, they’re perfect for quick bites, chai breaks, or nostalgic cravings.

Story About Us

We believe in family-friendly fun, flavor-rich recipes, and creating a hangout you’ll always return to.
